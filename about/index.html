Background

The work I do and why I do it

I started Olivia Arkema Creative because I kept seeing the same problem: small businesses doing great work in the real world, but struggling to show it online. A bad website — or no website at all — costs you customers you never even know you lost.

My background is a mix of web design, digital marketing, and data science. That combination means I don't just make things look good — I think about what your customers are looking for, how they find you, and what makes them reach out. Every decision I make is grounded in that.

I've worked with agriculture businesses, healthcare organizations, restaurants, service contractors, and independent professionals. The industries vary, but the core challenge is almost always the same: connecting a real, trustworthy business with the people who need it.

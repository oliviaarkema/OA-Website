What to Look For

What Makes a Good Trades Website?

📍

It Shows Up in Google

A well-built website helps Google understand who you are, what services you offer, and where you work—so local customers can find you when it counts.

⭐

It Looks Professional

Potential customers often decide within seconds whether they trust a business. A clean, professional website builds confidence before they ever pick up the phone.

📱

It Works on Mobile

Most people searching for contractors are doing it from their phones. Your website needs to look great and work smoothly on every device.

📞

It Makes Contact Easy

If customers have to hunt for your phone number, they'll often move on to the next company. A good website makes it easy to call, request a quote, or submit a form.

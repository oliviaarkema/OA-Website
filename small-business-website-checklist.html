Small Business Website Checklist

By Olivia Arkema · January 23, 2025

Your website is one of your most important tools for growing your small business. It should make a great first impression, guide visitors seamlessly, and help turn them into customers.

Focus on clear branding, a mobile-friendly design, and simple navigation to create a user-friendly experience. Make sure your website loads quickly, features accurate contact details, and has strong calls-to-action like "Get a Quote" or "Shop Now." Link to your Google Business Profile to improve local visibility, add social media links, and optimize your content with relevant keywords for SEO. Don't forget to secure your site with an SSL certificate and use analytics tools to measure performance and refine your strategy.
